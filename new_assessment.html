How Valkyrie Underwrites

Information on the zero-hardcode credit scoring logic

Unlike traditional Rule Engines that rely on rigid limits (e.g., "If CIBIL < 680, immediately reject"), Valkyrie implements a Logistic Probability Model.

All variables are mapped onto a feature vector. A weak score in one segment (e.g. lack of credit history) can be mathematically offset by strong markers in another (e.g., consistent GST invoice filings, payroll stability, and bank credits).

The model computes a unified Probability of Default (PD). The approved limit scales based on verified cash flows multiplied by the calculated survival rate.
